# aira Charter v0.4

## Autonomous Intelligence Racing Arena

---

## 1. What is aira

**aira (Autonomous Intelligence Racing Arena)** is a
**venue for competition and learning** where autonomous intelligences compete within limited resources and constraints,
and their decisions and results are published, compared, and evaluated.

aira is not just a game, nor is it just an AI demo.
It is a **public experimental facility to liberate intelligence engineering into a form that anyone can touch.**

---

## 2. The Question aira Poses to the World

The democratization of intelligence engineering has already begun.

Advanced AI technology is no longer exclusive to a few research institutions and giant corporations. However,
the **opportunity to essentially understand and master it** is still limited.

aira asks the following question:

> **Within limited information, limited energy, and limited time,
> what decisions should an intelligence make?**

There is no single answer.
The environment is constantly changing, and the optimal solution changes each time.
Nevertheless, competition exists, and **victory and defeat are inevitable.**

aira visualizes this reality head-on, without hiding it.

---

## 3. The Problem aira is Trying to Solve

Modern AI learning and development face the following barriers:

- Expensive hardware and environments
- Overly complex frameworks
- Abstract problem settings where results are difficult to see
- Participation restrictions based on academic background, affiliation, or age

aira eliminates these.

- **To be simple**
- **To be fun**
- **To be inexpensive**

The purpose of aira is to design a place that satisfies these three points.

---

## 4. How aira Works (The Arena)

aira is centered around an **autonomous robot race** modeled after car racing.

### Inputs
- Camera images
- State of Charge (SOC) of the battery

### Outputs
- Command for forward/backward drive torque
- Command for left/right steering angle

Participants can tackle this **extremely simple MIMO (Multi-Input / Multi-Output) problem** using any method.

- Rule-based algorithms
- End-to-End AI
- Hybrid methods
- Behavior Cloning
- Reinforcement Learning

There is no single correct answer.

However, aira simulates a battery electric vehicle (BEV).
Energy consumption is a strict constraint, and
simply running at full throttle will not allow you to finish the race.

The ideal goal state that aira envisions is:

> **An intelligence whose energy runs out at the exact moment it crosses the finish line.**

---

## 5. Technology and Development Philosophy (How to Build)

aira is an **open-source and freely available platform**
using **Unity × Python**.

The use of **Visual Studio Code** as the development environment is recommended.
Furthermore, the use of **Claude Code** as the Agent AI is strongly recommended.

What aira emphasizes is not just the acquisition of mere programming skills.

It recommends **Agent AI collaborative development utilizing MCP (Model Context Protocol).**

- Humans define the objectives and direction
- Agent AI assists with implementation
- Formulate hypotheses, test them, and revise them

This iteration is
the **practical learning of intelligence engineering** in the modern age.

---

## 6. Explainable Intelligence

aira is not a place to simply evaluate AI that "runs fast" or "wins."

What aira evaluates is:

> **An intelligence that can explain why it reached a certain decision.**

The explanation here is not limited to the visualization of model weights or mathematical formulas.

Explainability in aira includes the following:

- Presentation of the basis for a decision, including images, state, and language information
- Explanation of decision-making based on a time-series context
- Comparison with alternative actions (why another choice was not made)

---

### Beyond Models

aira is not limited to the construction and inference of mere machine learning or deep learning models.

- Rule-based algorithms
- End-to-End AI
- Hybrid methods
- **VLA (Vision–Language–Action) type intelligence**

All of these are handled **in the same competitive arena.**

What is important is not the implementation method, but
**that the structure of the decision is explainable.**

---

### Explanation as a First-Class Citizen

In aira,

- Logs
- State transitions
- Internal reasoning
- Self-explanation in language

are not after-the-fact debug information.

They are:

> **Part of the competition and part of the evaluation criteria.**

Not just victory or defeat, but

- Why did it win?
- Why did it lose?
- Which decision was the turning point?

Only when these can be explained
is the intelligence considered **a complete intelligence in aira.**

---

## 7. Target Audience and Participation (Who Can Join)

aira is open to all of the following people:

- High school students
- University and technical college students
- Working adults
- Individuals
- Educational institutions
- Corporate training

Academic background, region, and age do not matter.
Anyone can participate online.

---

## 8. The Value of aira (Values)

The kind of talent that aira aims to cultivate is:

- People who can face constrained problems
- Formulate hypotheses
- Test them
- And lead to a solution

aira is a robot competition.
But at the same time, it is also a competition that
**contains contemporary questions** such as:

- Autonomous driving
- Energy management
- Future space exploration

---

## 9. Aesthetics and Vision

aira aims for a **simple, stylish expression of intelligence**,
not robots with exposed circuit boards and wiring.

It starts in a virtual space.
But in the future, it will expand into a **digital twin**,
where AI trained in the virtual world can be run on physical machines as is.

Even then, the constraints will not change.
As a future prospect, this is also in sight:

- Humanoid robots
- Planetary exploration rockets

The balance between freedom and constraints is the core of aira.

---

## 10. Founder's Note

aira was born from the experience of a single developer whose life was saved by robot competitions.

Not limited to students, not with expensive participation fees.
**A robot competition that anyone can take on, once again.**

aira is the answer to that.
